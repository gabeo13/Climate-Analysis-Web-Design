Summary: Latitude vs. X

This webpage represents the results of a high-level meteorological analysis relative to equatorial bearing in the context of either vacation planning or general travel. Data for this project has been sourced from the OpenWeatherAPI, and may be found in the respective 'Data' page.

This analysis reviews specific relationships and trends between Latitude and the following: Cloudiness, Temperature, Wind Speed, and Humidity. All figures have been produced using the Matplotlib module within Python 3 and have been colored according to their respective target variable.
