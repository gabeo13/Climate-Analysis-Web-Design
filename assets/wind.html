Latitude vs. Wind Speed

Wind speed data appears to be very evenly balanced in the northern hemisphere around 8mph, increasing quite marginally with latitude. Wind speeds in the southern hemisphere however, increase mildly from equator to south pole.

Overall, the equatorial region appears to have lower wind speeds, making the area most ideal for travelers seeking a more static climate for their holidays. With blazing temperatures at the equator however, some sacrifice to the wind may need to be made to find perfect spot.
