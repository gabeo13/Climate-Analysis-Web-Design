Latitude vs. Cloudiness

While the data shows a fairly even distribution overall, cloud cover increases from south pole to north pole. Perhaps as an effect of seasonal influence or perhaps more data is needed in the southern hemisphere. This data has been collecting during January 2021 and thus winter in the northern hemisphere.

We must not rule out the fact that the southern hemisphere has far fewer major population centers and is likely producing a smaller data stream to the API. A more detailed, perhaps multi-variant analysis should be performed to research the relationship between Latitude and Cloudiness.
