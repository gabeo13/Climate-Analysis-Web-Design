Latitude vs. Max Temperature

Warmer temperatures in the southern hemisphere reflect the fact that summer has arrived. Parabolic curvature (Max Global Temperature) in the data peaks around the equator at 0 deg latitude. Strong indication that temperatures increase toward the equator in the Northern Hemisphere.

Seasonal influence in Southern Hemisphere dampens the slope, but analagous trend remains visible. For those seeking more temperature climates for their travels, 20 degress either north or south of the equator appear to be most ideal.
